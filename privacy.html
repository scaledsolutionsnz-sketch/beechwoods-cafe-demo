Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

Beechwoods Cafe respects your privacy. This policy explains how we handle information collected through this website. It is placeholder content provided for the website demo and should be reviewed before going live.

1. Information we collect

We only collect the information you choose to give us through our contact and feedback forms. This may include:

We use the information you provide to respond to your enquiry, manage bookings, and improve our service. We do not sell or rent your personal information to third parties.

Our forms are processed by a third-party form service that delivers your message to us by email. By submitting a form you consent to your details being sent for that purpose.

4. Cookies and analytics

This website may use basic cookies or analytics to understand how the site is used and to keep it working properly. You can disable cookies in your browser settings if you prefer.

5. Keeping your information safe

We take reasonable steps to protect the information you share with us and keep it only as long as needed to respond to you or as required by law.
