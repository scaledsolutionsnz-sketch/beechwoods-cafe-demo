In the heart of Murchison
Our place

A welcoming stop in the middle of it all

Beechwoods Cafe sits right on the way through Murchison — the little Buller town between the Lewis Pass and the West Coast where everyone seems to stop for a break. We're the kind of place where the coffee's good, the food's hearty and nobody minds if you've got muddy boots or a carload of kids.

Whether you're a traveller breaking up the drive, a local after your usual, or a group off the river, you'll get a warm welcome and a feed that does the job.

  • Locally owned and run, right here in Murchison
  • Coffee, breakfast and lunch served all day
  • Easy highway parking for cars, vans and trailers
